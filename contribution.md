# Contributing to Note

Large contributions to note are discouraged. Since this is a personal collection of
a thousand things I specifically want available in all my projects, I am unlikely to add
and support features I don't want. 

But I wholeheartedly thank you for caring enough to check the
contributions file, discouraging contributions is done with no malice, and I hope Note's direction
continues to benefit you and your projects.

Bug fixes and suggestions are wholly welcome, if they are small and do not have AI
LLM generated content.

Issues explaining a problem is very appreciated, and even more appreciated with a repro project.
